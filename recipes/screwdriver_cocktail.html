Screwdriver Cocktail

Description

This simple mixture of vodka and orange juice reportedly gets its name from the original tool used to stir the drink — a screwdriver. You'll get equally good results using a spoon. This screwdriver is an easy but refreshing cocktail recipe made by combining orange juice and vodka over ice.

  • 6 fluid ounces orange juice
  • 1 ½ fluid ounces vodka
  • 1 ½ cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine orange juice and vodka in a highball glass and stir to combine.
  3. Add ice. Enjoy!
